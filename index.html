Hey! Thanks for dropping by my website. My name is Lucas and this page will serve as an introduction to me and some of my hobbies/interests as well as providing a way for me to practice web design.

I grew up in the rainy state of Washington on the West Coast of the United States. As a kid in a small town my friends and I got up to all the usual shenanigans you might expect. Some favorite memories of mine are summer days spent riding around on our bikes down to the river or lake to spend the day fishing, the night was then spent playing video games or watching movies. In the winter I could be found skiing in Whitepass or Mt. Hood whenever possible.

After graduating high school in 2008 I joined the Air Force and have hardly been home since. I am currently stationed in Germany for the second time, I have also been stationed in the UK, California (where I met my wife), and Turkey. We hope to retire somewhere in the Pacific Northwest so hopefully my last assignment will take me that direction. Aside from that, I have a pretty wide variety of interests. I enjoy going to music festivals, painting miniatures, and traveling in general.
